## Summary

MOFLON is a Shenzhen, China-headquartered manufacturer of industrial slip rings and rotary unions, founded in 1995, offering an unusually broad customizable product line (miniature, capsule, through-bore, servo-encoder, Ethernet, high-frequency, wind turbine, and military-grade variants) across a wide range of industries including robotics, pan-tilt camera systems, radar, wind turbines, packaging machinery, and medical devices.

## Key Facts

- Founded: 1995, headquartered in Shenzhen, China
- Type: Component/subsystem supplier — industrial slip ring and rotary union manufacturer
- Status: Active, privately held; approximately 700 employees (per company materials), 20,000 square meter production base
- Key metric(s): Over 10,000 mature slip ring design drawings per company materials; over 80% of product models are customized to specific customer requirements; product specifications span up to IP68 sealing, up to 20,000 RPM operating speed, up to 450°C temperature resistance, and slip ring diameters up to 2 meters for large custom units
- Value chain position: Component/subsystem supplier (slip rings sold to robotics, camera/gimbal, radar, and general industrial OEMs and integrators worldwide — not a platform vendor)

## What It Is / How It Works

MOFLON's product line is organized around mounting configuration and application (through-bore, capsule, miniature, power, rotor-flange, stator-flange, servo-encoder, waterproof, Ethernet, industrial-bus, wind turbine, HD-SDI, fiber-optic rotary joint, and high-frequency slip rings), with contact structures spanning carbon brushes, gold wire contacts, gold fiber brush contacts, and non-contact transmission depending on the application's noise, wear, and lifespan requirements. The company explicitly lists robotics, pan-tilt cameras, and radar among its named application areas, alongside a much broader set of general industrial uses (wind turbine pitch control, packaging equipment, medical devices, semiconductor manufacturing, motion simulators).

Unlike Fabricast's narrower defense/aerospace-grade positioning, MOFLON competes primarily on customization breadth and production scale — the company reports more than 10,000 existing design drawings and states over 80% of shipped product models are customized per customer specification, backed by an ISO 9001-certified 20,000 square meter production facility staffed by over 700 employees. The company also lists "military grade" slip rings as a distinct product line, indicating some defense-market positioning alongside its broader commercial/industrial customer base.
