## Overview

US companies hold the top private valuations in humanoid robotics but lag Chinese manufacturers significantly on unit volume. As of mid-2026, Agility Robotics is the only US company with sustained commercial deployment in paying customer facilities. Tesla Optimus and Figure AI have larger public profiles but remain in internal testing or early trials.
