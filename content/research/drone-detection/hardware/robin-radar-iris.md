## Summary

Robin Radar Systems (The Hague, Netherlands) produces the IRIS radar — a 3D 360° pulse-Doppler radar with micro-Doppler classification purpose-built for drone detection. Robin Radar's origin is in bird detection radars for airport birdstrike prevention, giving it an unusually strong foundation in the exact problem that plagues drone detection: discriminating drones from birds. IRIS combines 360° elevation/azimuth coverage with AI micro-Doppler classification to distinguish drones, birds, aircraft, and clutter in a single sensor.

## Key Facts

- **Manufacturer:** Robin Radar Systems, The Hague, Netherlands
- **Type:** Company — Radar hardware + software
- **Product:** IRIS 3D 360° radar
- **Coverage:** 360° azimuth; 3D (azimuth + elevation + range)
- **Technology:** Pulse-Doppler + micro-Doppler AI classification
- **Application heritage:** Airport bird detection radars (40+ airport deployments)
- **Status:** Active; commercial deployments

## What Makes IRIS Distinct

Robin Radar's competitive differentiation is its institutional knowledge of bird-vs-drone discrimination. The company built its business on airport avian radars that must reliably detect birds to prevent birdstrike — the exact clutter problem that hampers C-UAS radar. This translates to:

- **AI classification trained on bird data:** Years of bird track data from airport deployments informs the drone classifier, making the bird rejection filter unusually robust
- **3D coverage in a single sensor:** Many radar systems require multiple units for full 3D coverage; IRIS achieves this in a single installation
- **On-the-move detection:** Datasheet indicates detection while moving, enabling mobile platform integration
- **Early warning orientation:** Designed for perimeter warning rather than fire control; appropriate for detection-and-alert critical infrastructure applications

## Limitations

- **Radar-only:** No RF, acoustic, or optical layer; requires integration with other sensors for full C-UAS picture
- **No countermeasures:** Detection and track only
- **Commercial specifications:** Full detection range, classification accuracy, and pricing not publicly available in accessible datasheets
